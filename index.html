G-SHOCK | WATCH

Let you shine with our watches.

Starting from the idea of ​​breaking conventional standards, the developer believes that creating a watch that is unbreakable, even if dropped, is possible, formed Project Team Tough in 1981 to make it happen. The team created more than 200 prototypes one after another in about two years. They finally found a shock-resistant structure. This event marked the birth of a powerful technology down to its internal components.

And G-SHOCK has continued to evolve since then, in order to find his perfect toughness in all aspects, from structure and material to function. Now, the challenges faced and continuous efforts the development team has produced results, namely Triple G Resist, tough structure that can withstand 3 kinds of gravitational acceleration.

In order for the watch to be worn under any circumstances, G-SHOCK installs Smart Access, a system that ensures convenient and stable operation. Getting stronger, until it becomes the strongest, G-SHOCK development will never end.
